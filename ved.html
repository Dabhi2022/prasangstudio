PRASANG Photography Studio was founded in Vadodara with a singular vision — to create imagery that moves people. Over nearly a decade, we have worked with hundreds of families, couples, and brands, building a reputation for soulful, technically flawless photography.

Our studio is equipped with state-of-the-art lighting and camera systems. But our greatest asset remains a deeply personal approach — taking time to understand each client and crafting visuals that feel true.
